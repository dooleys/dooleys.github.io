Samuel Dooley

Senior Research Scientist at Abaus.AI.

Email GoogleScholar Twitter Linkedin

I am a human-centered machine learning researcher where I develop large-scale, production systems and research how they impact individuals. I work on technologies like Neural Architecture Search (NAS), Hyperparameter Optimization (HPO), Large Language Models, and Forecasting.

I have a PhD in Computer Science from University of Maryland, a Master's in Statistics from George Washington University, and a Bachelor's in Mathematics from University of Chicago.

I have published works in NeurIPS (🎉award winning🎉), IJCAI, CHI (🎉award winning🎉), FAccT, AIES, and PoPETs; and my work has been covered in popular press at Scientific American, WIRED, and VentureBeat.
